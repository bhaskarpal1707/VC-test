About Vintage Creation

"Every frame should carry the weight of a feeling."

Vintage Creation is a Kolkata-based creative studio founded by a self-made visual editor and colorist. With a deep understanding of storytelling, mood, and brand identity, we transform raw footage into cinematic narratives.

From digital campaigns to brand films, every project is crafted with precision, emotion, and a strong visual language. We work with national and regional brands delivering consistent quality and creative direction.
